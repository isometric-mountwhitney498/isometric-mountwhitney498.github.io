📖 My Story

From Curious Newcomer to Display Expert

Hi, I'm Barry. In March 2024, I joined Riotouch Technology as a Regional Sales Manager with a simple mission: help businesses and educators find the right display solutions without the confusion that usually comes with tech purchases.

Before Riotouch, I spent years observing how display technology was transforming classrooms and retail spaces worldwide. I saw too many buyers make expensive decisions based on incomplete information — and I wanted to change that.

At Riotouch, I found the perfect match: a company that builds Plug & Play products designed for real people, not just engineers. Our LED displays power on and play. Our IFP panels come with intuitive Android-based Daisy OS. No technician needed — just plug in and go.

Since joining, I've traveled to 15+ countries — from Eastern Europe to Southeast Asia, from South Asia to China's Canton Fair. Every trip taught me something new about what customers actually need versus what they think they need.

This website is my way of sharing that knowledge with you. Whether you're a school administrator looking for interactive panels, a retailer wanting eye-catching LED posters, or a business owner exploring digital signage — I'm here to help you make the right choice.

Let's make display technology simple together.
