❓ FAQ

Questions from Real Buyers

Based on 2 years of customer conversations

❓Is your Smartboard like a TV also?
▼
"Kind of but Price higher. Can you play video and write on it?"
❓What's the difference between RK3588 and RK3576?
▼
RK3576 is cheaper and has middle specs. The RK3588 is the flagship with more power. Tell me your use case, I'll recommend the right one.
❓How long to ship samples?
▼
Inventory ready for shipping. Most sizes in stock. Confirm size and I'll tell you exact lead time.
❓Do you have the required certifications?
▼
IEC 62368-1 / SANS 62368-1 (safety), EMC compliance, RF approvals, CE, FCC, RoHS. All export-ready.
❓What is the HS code for customs?
▼
HS 8471.41.4000 — for Interactive Flat Panels / Smartboards. LED displays may vary.
❓Can I pay through Alibaba Trade Assurance?
▼
Alibaba it's fine. Or bank transfer T/T for direct orders. I'll guide you whichever works.
❓What warranty do you offer?
▼
1 year for panel, 3 years for core components (motherboard, power board). All products exceed industry LED standards.
❓Do you customize shapes like spheres or curves?
▼
Yes — Sphere, Can, Cylinder, Christmas Tree, custom curves. Let me know your size and pitch preference.
