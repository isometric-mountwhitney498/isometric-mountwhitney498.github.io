LCD vs LED — What's the Real Difference?

Understanding this one concept unlocks everything else.

The single biggest difference: LCD pixels are lit by a backlight, while LED pixels produce their own light. That one change creates everything else — brightness, black levels, shape flexibility.

CHAPTER 2

6 Key Specs You Should Know

Don't worry — it's simpler than it sounds.

📐

Pixel Pitch (P1.25, P1.53, P2.5...)

The distance between LED centers, measured in millimeters. Smaller pitch = sharper image = closer viewing distance. Rule of thumb: minimum viewing distance (meters) ≈ pitch number (mm). So P2.5 looks sharp from 2.5m away.

💡 Tell us your viewing distance → we recommend the right pitch
📸

Refresh Rate (3840Hz / 7680Hz)

How fast the screen refreshes per second. In LED, this matters for photography — not video smoothness. 3840Hz means smartphone photos look perfect with no scan lines. 7680Hz = broadcast / live-stream grade.

💡 If customers photograph your ads → 3840Hz minimum
🛡️

GOB Coating (Glue on Board)

A transparent resin layer applied over LED beads. Makes the surface water-resistant and impact-resistant — like a built-in screen protector. Essential for retail, events, and anywhere people can touch the screen.

💡 Ask: "Is GOB included?" — some suppliers skip it to cut price
🔌

Plug & Play

Power on → content playing. No installation technician, no wall mounting, no software configuration. Your store clerk can set it up in 2 minutes. All Riotouch LED products are Plug & Play by design.

💡 Zero installation cost = faster ROI
📱

APP Content Management

Change your display content from a free mobile app via WiFi. Head office can push ads to all branch locations simultaneously. No monthly CMS subscription fee — the app is included free.

💡 Free app vs paid CMS = significant cost saving
🌧️

IP Rating (IP65)

IP65 = dust-tight + survives rain and water jets. All Riotouch outdoor products are IP65 rated. Indoor products use GOB coating instead — protects against coffee spills, dust, and accidental bumps.

💡 Outdoor = must have IP65; Indoor = GOB is enough

CHAPTER 5

Frequently Asked Questions

Real questions from real buyers.

Q Is LED more expensive than LCD?
It depends on the use case. For indoor close-range display, LCD can be cheaper upfront. But for outdoor use, LED is the only option — LCD simply cannot achieve the brightness needed for sunlight visibility.
  • Lifespan: LED lasts 80,000-100,000 hours vs LCD's 30,000-50,000 hours
  • Total Cost of Ownership: When you factor in lifespan, installation cost (Plug & Play = free), and no CMS subscription fee, LED often costs less over 5 years
Ask us for a TCO comparison tailored to your project.
Q Do I need a technician to install it?
No. All Riotouch products are Plug & Play — plug into a power outlet, and your content starts playing. No wall mounting, no software setup, no technician. Your store staff can handle it in 2 minutes. Content changes are done via free mobile APP.
Q What happens if an LED bead breaks?
Three layers of protection:
  • GOB coating — transparent resin over all beads, resistant to water, dust, and impact
  • Magnetic front maintenance — if a module fails, pop it out and replace it in 5 minutes, no tools needed
  • Gray scale masking — even if 1-2 individual beads fail, they're invisible at normal viewing distance
Module replacement is affordable — ask us for spare module pricing.
Q How do I change the content on the screen?
Free mobile APP via WiFi. Upload images/videos from your phone, schedule playlists, push content to multiple locations simultaneously. No monthly subscription — the app is included free with every Riotouch product. Works on both iOS and Android.
Q Can I connect multiple screens together?
Yes. Our foldable LED posters and outdoor screens support multi-unit splicing — connect 2, 4, or more units to create a seamless large display. The content management APP handles multi-screen layouts automatically.
Q What warranty do you offer?
Standard warranty: 1-year整机 (整机) + 3-year配件 (配件) covering module replacement. Extended warranty options available. Our products are certified: ISO9001, CE, RoHS, FCC. All products exceed industry LED display standards.
Q I'm comparing quotes from multiple suppliers. What should I check?
Make sure you're comparing apples to apples. Key items to verify:
  • Refresh rate: Is 3840Hz included, or just 1920Hz? (1920Hz shows scan lines in photos)
  • GOB coating: Is it included or extra? (Some suppliers skip it to lower the price)
  • Content APP: Is it included free, or is there a monthly subscription?
  • Certifications: CE, RoHS, FCC — are they real and current?
  • Warranty terms: What's covered and for how long?
We're happy to provide a detailed spec-for-spec comparison — just ask.
